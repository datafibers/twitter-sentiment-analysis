RWC2015 Tweet-eater

This is a simple app that will connect to the Twitter sample "firehose" streaming API and then just look for tweets related to RWC2015 and save them by country mentioned.

Built using Sprinb-Boot, Reactor and Redis.

You aren't connected to Twitter yet. Click the button to connect this application with your Twitter account.
